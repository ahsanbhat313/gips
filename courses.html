Our Paramedical Programs

Launch your career in healthcare with our industry-recognized courses that combine theory with intensive practical training.

GIPS offers a diverse range of undergraduate and diploma programs essential for the modern medical team. Our curriculum is designed by experts and approved by relevant statutory bodies to ensure our students are job-ready and highly skilled.

Featured Paramedical Disciplines

BSc Operation Theatre Technology

Focuses on managing the OT equipment and assisting surgeons during operations. A critical role in surgical success.

Duration: 3 Years + 6 Months Internship

Eligibility: 10+2 Science (Physics, Chemistry, Biology)

Career Path: OT Technician, Surgical Assistant

Apply Now

General Nursing and Midwifery (GNM)

A comprehensive program preparing students for direct patient care, hospital management, and community health.

Duration: 3 Years

Eligibility: 10+2 (Any Stream, preferably Science)

Career Path: Staff Nurse, Community Health Worker

Apply Now

BSc Medical Lab Technology

Involves the diagnosis, treatment, and prevention of disease through clinical laboratory tests.

Duration: 3 Years

Eligibility: 10+2 Science (Physics, Chemistry, Biology)

Career Path: Lab Technologist, Research Assistant

Apply Now

Diploma in Anaesthesia Technology

Specialized training in monitoring patients and assisting the Anaesthesiologist during surgical procedures.

Duration: 2 Years

Eligibility: 10+2 Science (Physics, Chemistry, Biology)

Career Path: Anaesthesia Technician
