World-Class Infrastructure

Our campus is designed to provide an optimal learning environment with modern laboratories and comfortable amenities.

At GIPS, we believe that practical training is the cornerstone of paramedical education. We have invested heavily in creating state-of-the-art facilities that mirror real-world clinical settings, ensuring our students gain hands-on experience from day one.

Key Facilities at a Glance

Modern Paramedical Laboratories

Our department-specific labs—including Anatomy, Physiology, Pathology, and Operation Theatre Technology labs—are fully equipped with the latest diagnostic and practical instruments.

  • Digital microscopes and advanced sterilization units.
  • Fully functional dummy setup for OT procedures.
  • Dedicated sections for MLT practicals (Haematology, Biochemistry).

Digital and Physical Library

The GIPS Library is the intellectual heart of the campus, housing a vast collection of paramedical journals, essential textbooks, and research papers.

  • Comfortable seating for over 100 students.
  • Subscription to national and international e-journals.
  • Dedicated digital resource area with high-speed internet.

Smart Classrooms & IT Centre

All classrooms are equipped with modern teaching aids, ensuring an interactive and engaging educational experience.

  • Audio-visual equipment (Projectors, Sound Systems).
  • High-speed Wi-Fi access across all academic blocks.
  • Dedicated Computer Lab for practical IT and research work.

Hostel, Canteen, and Transport

We provide comfortable and secure residential facilities and efficient transport for the convenience of our students.

  • Separate, secure hostel facilities for male and female students.
  • Hygienic and spacious cafeteria serving nutritious meals.
  • Dedicated bus fleet for timely transport to clinical postings and main routes.

Experience GIPS Firsthand

We encourage prospective students and parents to visit our campus to fully appreciate the infrastructure and facilities dedicated to healthcare education.
