Admission Procedure 2024-25

Your guide to joining the Guru Institute of Paramedical Sciences.

✓ Eligibility Criteria

Candidates must meet the following minimum requirements to be considered for admission into our Paramedical Diploma and Degree courses:

  • **Academic Qualification:** Candidate must have successfully completed 10+2 (Higher Secondary) or an equivalent examination from a recognized board.
  • **Subject Requirement:** For most courses (like Operation Theatre Technology and Medical Lab Technology), candidates must have studied **Science** (Physics, Chemistry, and Biology/Math) as compulsory subjects in 10+2.
  • **Minimum Marks:** A minimum aggregate score of **45%** (40% for reserved categories) in the qualifying examination is required.
  • **Age Limit:** The candidate must be at least **17 years of age** as of December 31st of the admission year.

*Specific course eligibility may vary. Please check the respective course pages for detailed criteria.

→ 5 Steps to Apply

1. Obtain Application Form

The application form can be collected directly from the institute office or downloaded from the official website (when available).

2. Fill and Submit

Complete the form accurately and submit it along with the required processing fee before the deadline.

3. Merit List / Interview

Admission is primarily based on academic merit. Shortlisted candidates may be called for a counseling session or a brief interview.

4. Document Verification

Candidates must bring all original documents and photocopies for verification on the specified date.

5. Fee Payment and Enrollment

Upon successful verification, secure your seat by paying the first installment of the tuition fee.

📄 Mandatory Documents

Please ensure you have both the original and two sets of photocopies of the following documents ready for verification:

  • 10th Class Mark Sheet and Certificate
  • 12th Class Mark Sheet and Provisional Certificate
  • Date of Birth Certificate (if not on 10th certificate)
  • Domicile Certificate
  • Character Certificate from previous institution
  • Migration/Transfer Certificate
  • Aadhar Card / ID Proof
  • 4 Passport Size Photographs
