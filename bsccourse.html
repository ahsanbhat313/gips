Bachelor of Science (B.Sc.) Programs

A strong foundation for clinical excellence. Full-time, three-year degree courses focused on specialized healthcare roles.

Our B.Sc. programs are designed to create highly skilled, analytical, and critical-thinking paramedical professionals. These degree courses emphasize in-depth theoretical knowledge combined with extensive clinical training in affiliated hospitals.

Degree Programs Offered

B.Sc. Operation Theatre Technology

Training focused on aseptic practices, instrumentation, and patient monitoring during pre, intra, and post-operative procedures.

Duration: 3 Years + Internship

Eligibility: 10+2 Science (PCB)

Details & Apply

B.Sc. Medical Lab Technology

Comprehensive study of clinical chemistry, haematology, microbiology, and pathology essential for disease diagnosis.

Duration: 3 Years

Eligibility: 10+2 Science (PCB)

Details & Apply

B.Sc. Renal Dialysis Technology

Specialized course on operating dialysis machinery, monitoring patients, and maintaining equipment for kidney care.

Duration: 3 Years

Eligibility: 10+2 Science (PCB)

Details & Apply

B.Sc. Radiology & Imaging Tech

Training in the use of sophisticated medical imaging techniques like X-ray, CT Scan, and MRI for diagnostic purposes.

Duration: 3 Years

Eligibility: 10+2 Science (PCB)

Details & Apply

Minimum Admission Requirement

Admission to all B.Sc. Paramedical courses requires a minimum of **10+2 (Higher Secondary)** qualification with **Physics, Chemistry, and Biology (PCB)** as core subjects from a recognized board.
